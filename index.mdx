Welcome to Rankly, the premier Discord server listing and discovery platform. Whether you're a server owner looking to grow your community or a bot developer integrating with Rankly, you'll find everything you need here.

## What is Rankly?

Rankly connects Discord communities with passionate users. Server owners list their servers, earn credits through engagement, and spend them on visibility features. Users discover amazing new servers and vote for their favorites.
